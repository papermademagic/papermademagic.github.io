Educational Services
As an Educational Services Representative, I am excited to offer a full range of services for your school or library. See below for the services I offer and additional resources.  If you represent a school or library in/near the Merrimack Valley of Massachusetts, I would love to work with you! If you are located elsewhere, please still feel free to contact me, and I will connect you with a representative in your area!

Book Fairs

Your school, library, or organization can earn cash or free books by hosting a book fair! In-person and online ordering available.

Classroom & Library Orders    

Order books directly for your classroom or library, and take advantage of our optional library-bound titles and cataloging products!

Literacy for a Lifetime™

You can stretch your grant dollars even further with our 50% book grant-matching program!

Reach For The Stars!

This pledge-based reading-incentive program is a great way to get students excited about reading, while earning cash and/or books for your organization and your students!

Cards for a Cause

A simple traditional fundraising program! Sell boxes of 30 premium greeting cards for $30, and keep $13 for your organization!
