My Story

In our family, we REALLY love books!
My first few years as a mom were tough.  I struggled with post-partum depression.  Curling up together with a book was my refuge - it was how I bonded with my baby, how I got through a long day with a rambunctious toddler, and how I reconnected with my preschooler after a meltdown. That brown chair - one that first belonged to my parents, and in which I spent a great deal of time reading as a child - is our special place! 

Why Usborne Books & More?   

It all started with two of my favorite words: "free books". I figured I'd host a party, get some free books, and be done with it.  Then I saw how much my child loves these books - and how well they withstood his loving! 
And I love the books, too! From detailed, engaging non-fiction, to gorgeously illustrated picture books, to road-trip-sanity-saving activity books, to YA novels even I can't put down, there's something for everyone - and I can't wait to help you find the ones that are right for the children in your life!
